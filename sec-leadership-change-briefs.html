Form 8-K - April 22, 2026

Regal Rexnord CEO Succession

CEO

Trigger: Aamir Paul will succeed Louis V. Pinkham as CEO no later than July 1, 2026, join the board, and Pinkham will leave officer/director roles.

Operational Signal

  • CEO succession with board and offer-letter detail can create onboarding, compensation-disclosure, governance-update, and stakeholder messaging work.
  • Item 7.01 and Item 9.01 exhibits add press-release and offer-letter context for comms and compensation teams.

Buyer Types

  • Executive search firms, leadership onboarding advisors, IR/comms teams, compensation consultants, governance counsel.
  • Suggested use: monitor proxy and compensation disclosures before preparing any account-specific transition note.
Source and compliance note

Source: Regal Rexnord Form 8-K. This brief does not assess the issuer, its securities, or future performance.

Form 8-K - April 14, 2026

The Coretec Group Board Departures

Board

Trigger: three board departures across April 10 and April 13, including vice chair, chair, and director resignations.

Operational Signal

  • Multiple board resignations can create board-refresh, committee-coverage, independence, governance-calendar, and stakeholder-communication needs.
  • The filing includes resignation-letter exhibits and states the departures were not due to disagreement with operations, policies, or practices.

Buyer Types

  • Board search firms, governance advisors, public-company counsel, IR/comms advisors, OTC issuer compliance vendors.
  • Suggested use: track follow-on board appointments and committee-coverage disclosures.
Source and compliance note

Source: The Coretec Group Form 8-K. This brief does not infer dispute, misconduct, or company quality beyond the public filing.

Form 8-K - April 8, 2026

Universal Logistics CFO Transition

CFO

Trigger: Jude M. Beres resigned as CFO/Treasurer effective May 29, 2026; Michael Rogers was appointed CFO/Treasurer effective June 1, 2026.

Operational Signal

  • CFO handoff can create close ownership, finance delegation, disclosure-control, banking/vendor authority, and onboarding-material work.
  • The filing says Beres will remain through May 29 to assist the transition and describes Rogers' compensation terms.

Buyer Types

  • Interim finance advisors, SOX/readiness consultants, controller services firms, executive onboarding advisors, IR/comms teams.
  • Suggested use: monitor employment-agreement exhibits and finance-leadership updates in the next periodic filing.
Source and compliance note

Source: Universal Logistics Form 8-K. This is an operations signal only.

Form 8-K - April 7, 2026

Kura Sushi CFO Search

Interim

Trigger: CFO/Treasurer Jeffrey J. Uttz resigned effective April 28, 2026; CEO Hajime Uba will serve as interim CFO/principal financial officer while a search runs.

Operational Signal

  • Interim CFO coverage during an active search can indicate search support, finance continuity, disclosure-control ownership, and audit/quarter-close handoff needs.
  • The same 8-K includes earnings-related disclosure and press-release exhibits, increasing communications context.

Buyer Types

  • Executive search firms, interim CFO providers, SOX/readiness advisors, restaurant-sector finance consultants, IR/comms teams.
  • Suggested use: monitor the permanent CFO appointment and quarterly filings before any account-specific action.
Source and compliance note

Source: Kura Sushi USA Form 8-K. This brief does not evaluate operating results, management quality, or securities value.

WW International Leadership Cluster

Cluster
